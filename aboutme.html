Being Syrian has shaped not only who I am, but also how I see the world. Growing up in a country known for its history, resilience, and cultural depth, I learned early on the value of perseverance. Life in Syria teaches you to adapt, to stay hopeful, and to build your future even when the circumstances around you are challenging. That mindset became a core part of my identity. It pushed me to pursue knowledge, to stay curious, and to believe that ambition can thrive anywhere — even in places where opportunities are not always easy to find.

My nationality is something I carry with pride. It reminds me that strength doesn’t always come from comfort; sometimes it comes from navigating difficulties and still choosing to dream big. As I grew older, I realized that technology — especially computer science — could become my way of contributing something meaningful, both to myself and to the world around me.
